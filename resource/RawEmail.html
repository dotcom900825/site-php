Your Digital Membership Card has Arrived.

Follow the instructions below to set up the digital membership card on your iPhone or Android phone.


Visit iPassStore.com for more digital content.

iPhone User

OS Requirement: iOS 6 or up

1. Set up your email account on your iPhone. 

2. Go to your Mail App, your Mail App should automatically recognize the email you received earlier contains a Passbook file.

3. Add the membership card to your Passbook manually by clicking the Add button at the upper-right corner.

4. Open your Passbook app now and you should see your CSSA membership card.

5. Click the little i symbol on the lower-right corner to view the contents on the back.

6. Congratulations, you are done. It's that simple. 

Android User

OS Requirement: Android 2.1 or up

1. Set up your Email in your Email App.

2. Go to Google Play Store and download the free app PassWallet.

3. Then open the email you just received inside your Email App and save the attachment.

4. Open your PassWallet App, now you should see your digital membership card.

5. Tap the Android action button at the lower-right corner of your screen.

6. Flip your card to view the contents at the back.

7. Congratulations, you are done. It's that simple.
